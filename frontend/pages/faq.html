FAQ

How do I submit my artwork?

You can submit your artwork through the artist submission page.

Does it cost money to submit?

No, submitting your artwork is completely free.

How long does it take for my artwork to appear?

Your artwork is reviewed and added within 1–3 days.

How do I update or remove my submission?

Contact the developer using the email listed in the footer.

Can anyone attend events?

Yes, everyone is welcome to join us at our events.
