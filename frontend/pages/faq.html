Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ArtConnect.

FAQ

How do I submit my artwork?

You can submit your artwork using the Artist Submission page. Fill out the form and upload an image of your work.

Does it cost money to submit?

No, submitting your artwork is completely free.

How long does it take for my artwork to appear?

Artwork is reviewed and added within 1–3 days.

Can I update or remove my submission?

Yes. Contact the developer using the email listed in the footer.
